Pop Blocks Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Pop Blocks is a casual puzzle game developed by RedOne. This privacy policy explains how the app handles information.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal information through Pop Blocks.

Game Center

If you use Game Center features such as leaderboards, Apple may process your Game Center account information according to Apple's privacy policy. We do not receive your Apple ID password or payment information.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Diagnostics

Apple may provide aggregated crash or performance information to help developers improve apps. We use this information only to diagnose and improve app stability.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us through the support page: https://redone2000.github.io/xiaoyi/popstar.html

Pop Blocks 不会通过 App 收集、存储、出售或分享个人信息。
