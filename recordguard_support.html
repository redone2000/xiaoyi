RecordGuard Support

RecordGuard is a Mac menu-bar utility that creates local backup audio tracks while you record with your normal recorder.

For bug reports or setup questions, please include your macOS version, Mac model, recording app, microphone route, and what you expected to happen.

Feedback

Feature requests, bug reports, and workflow feedback are welcome by email.

If possible, include:

  • what you were recording with, such as QuickTime, OBS, Loom, Zoom, or ScreenFlow;
  • what you expected RecordGuard to do;
  • what happened;
  • whether system.m4a worked;
  • whether microphone.m4a worked;
  • the Result Audit text, if available.

Please do not send sensitive audio recordings unless specifically requested.

Basic Setup

RecordGuard needs two macOS permissions:

  • Screen & System Audio Recording, for system-audio capture.
  • Microphone, for microphone capture.

If audio is missing, open RecordGuard's setup check and confirm both permissions are enabled in macOS System Settings.

What RecordGuard Creates

Each run saves local files on your Mac:

  • system.m4a
  • microphone.m4a
  • session.json

RecordGuard does not upload these files.

Intended Use

Use RecordGuard with ordinary, non-sensitive content first to confirm that your Mac, microphone, and recording route behave as expected.

RecordGuard is not a screen recorder, is not designed for hidden recording, and does not guarantee recovery of every failed recording.

基础设置

RecordGuard 需要两个 macOS 权限：

  • 屏幕与系统音频录制权限，用于捕获系统音频。
  • 麦克风权限，用于捕获麦克风音频。

如果缺少音频，请打开 RecordGuard 的 setup check，并确认两个权限已在 macOS 系统设置中启用。

RecordGuard 会创建什么

每次运行会在你的 Mac 本地保存文件：

  • system.m4a
  • microphone.m4a
  • session.json

RecordGuard 不会上传这些文件。
