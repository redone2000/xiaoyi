Get 1024 Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Get 1024 is a casual puzzle game developed by RedOne. This privacy policy explains how the app handles information.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal information through Get 1024.

Get 1024 may save game progress, settings, and high score information locally on your device. This information is used only to provide the game experience and is not sent to us.

Get 1024 does not require account login. The app does not include third-party advertising, third-party analytics, or tracking technologies.

The current version of Get 1024 does not use Game Center features or in-app purchases.

Diagnostics

Apple may provide aggregated crash or performance information to help developers improve apps. We use this information only to diagnose issues and improve app stability.

Contact
