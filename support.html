订阅与自动续订说明 / Subscription Information

应用内提供自动续订订阅服务：
The app offers auto-renewable subscriptions:

  • 订阅会自动续订，除非用户在当前周期结束前至少 24 小时关闭自动续订。
    Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • 购买确认时，费用将从您的 Apple ID 账户扣除。
    Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase.
  • 用户可在购买后前往账户设置管理或取消订阅。
    You can manage or cancel your subscription in your account settings after purchase.
  • 如提供免费试用，试用期结束后将自动转为付费订阅。
    If a free trial is offered, it will automatically convert to a paid subscription after the trial ends.
