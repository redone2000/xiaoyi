FluxPipe Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

FluxPipe is a puzzle game about connecting matching colors. This privacy policy explains how the iOS app handles data.

Data We Do Not Collect

FluxPipe does not require an account, phone number, real name, or payment information to play.

The iOS version does not include third-party advertising SDKs or cross-app tracking SDKs.

Local Game Data

The app stores game progress, settings, sound preferences, daily warm-up progress, scores, and similar gameplay state on your device.

This local data remains on your device unless you delete the app, reset device data, or use a feature that sends gameplay content to an online service.

Workshop, Scores, and Shared Content

If you use online workshop, weekly rush, or sharing-related features, the app may send gameplay data needed for those features, such as published level data, creator display name, level identifiers, play counts, clear counts, likes, scores, and challenge/share metadata.

Published workshop levels and creator names are intended to be visible to other players. Do not publish content or names that include private or sensitive information.

Sharing

When you use the iOS share sheet, Apple and the selected destination app handle the sharing flow. FluxPipe only prepares the share text or image for the action you started.

Support and Feedback

If you email feedback or support details, the information you include in that email will be received through your email provider and used for support and product-improvement purposes.

Please do not send passwords, verification codes, identity documents, payment information, or other sensitive content.

Third-Party Services

FluxPipe may use platform services provided by Apple for app distribution, sharing, diagnostics, and system permissions.

Online gameplay features may use server infrastructure operated for FluxPipe to store and retrieve workshop levels, scores, and related gameplay records.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be published at this URL.

Contact

For questions or support, contact: fluxpipe.game@outlook.com

卢比克斯管道隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 3 日

《卢比克斯管道》是一款连接同色管道的益智游戏。本隐私政策说明 iOS 版本如何处理数据。

我们不会收集的数据

《卢比克斯管道》游玩时不要求注册账号、手机号、真实姓名或支付信息。

iOS 版本不包含第三方广告 SDK，也不包含跨 App 追踪 SDK。

本地游戏数据

App 会在你的设备本地保存游戏进度、设置、声音偏好、每日热身进度、分数等游戏状态。

这些本地数据会保留在你的设备上，除非你删除 App、清除设备数据，或主动使用会把游戏内容发送到在线服务的功能。

工坊、分数与分享内容

如果你使用在线关卡工坊、每周冲榜或分享相关功能，App 可能会发送这些功能所需的游戏数据，例如发布的关卡数据、创作者显示名称、关卡编号、游玩次数、通关次数、点赞数、分数，以及挑战或分享相关记录。

已发布的工坊关卡和创作者名称设计上会对其他玩家可见。请不要发布包含私人或敏感信息的内容或名称。

分享

当你使用 iOS 系统分享面板时，Apple 和你选择的目标 App 会处理后续分享流程。《卢比克斯管道》只会为你主动发起的分享动作准备分享文字或图片。

支持与反馈

如果你通过邮件发送反馈或支持信息，你在邮件中包含的信息会通过你的邮件服务提供商发送，并用于支持和产品改进。

请不要发送密码、验证码、身份证件、支付信息或其他敏感内容。

第三方服务

《卢比克斯管道》可能会使用 Apple 提供的平台服务，用于 App 分发、系统分享、诊断和系统权限。

在线玩法功能可能会使用《卢比克斯管道》运营的服务器基础设施，用于保存和读取工坊关卡、分数及相关游戏记录。

政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新内容将发布在此网址。

联系方式

如有问题或需要支持，请联系：fluxpipe.game@outlook.com
