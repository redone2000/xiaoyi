一、我们如何收集和使用您的个人信息 / How We Collect and Use Your Information

我们仅在提供服务所必需的范围内收集和使用您的个人信息，包括账户注册、订阅购买、订单校验、功能使用日志等。

We collect and use your personal information only to the extent necessary to provide our services, including account registration, subscription purchases, receipt validation, and usage logs.

（一）账户与基础功能 / Account and Basic Features

当您注册或登录时，我们可能收集您的手机号、昵称、头像（如您选择上传）等信息，用于身份识别与安全保障。

When you register or log in, we may collect information such as your phone number, nickname, and profile picture (if uploaded) to identify you and ensure account security.

（二）订阅/内购相关信息 / Subscription & In-App Purchase Information

当您通过应用商店购买订阅时，我们会接收必要的订单票据、订阅状态、到期时间等信息，用于开通或续期您的权益。支付由平台处理，我们不存储您的银行卡号。

When you purchase a subscription through the app store, we receive necessary order receipts, subscription status, and expiration dates to activate or renew your benefits. Payments are processed by the platform, and we do not store your card details.

二、我们如何共享、转让与公开披露个人信息 / How We Share, Transfer, and Disclose Information

除非取得您的同意或法律法规要求，我们不会与第三方共享您的个人信息。订阅相关数据仅在必要范围内与应用商店共享。

We will not share your personal information with third parties unless with your consent or as required by law. Subscription-related data is shared only with app stores within the necessary scope.

三、您的权利 / Your Rights

您有权访问、更正、删除您的个人信息，并可随时通过应用内设置或联系我们撤回授权。

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information, and you may withdraw your consent at any time via in-app settings or by contacting us.
