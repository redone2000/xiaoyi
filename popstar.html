About the Game

Pop Blocks is a simple tap-to-clear puzzle game designed for calm, repeatable play. Tap two or more blocks of the same color to clear them. The bigger the group, the higher your score.

When the board gets tight, use 3x3 clear or color clear boosters to keep the game moving.

关于游戏

Pop Blocks 是一款轻松的点击消除方块益智游戏，适合随时玩一局。点击两个或更多同色方块即可消除，一次消除越多，得分越高。

遇到卡点时，可以使用 3x3 清除或同色清除道具，帮助你继续推进关卡。
