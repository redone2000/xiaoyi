RecordGuard Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026

RecordGuard is a Mac utility that creates local backup audio tracks during recording workflows. This privacy policy explains how RecordGuard handles data.

Data Collection

RecordGuard does not collect personal data.

The app does not require an account, does not use analytics, does not use advertising trackers, and does not upload audio files to a server.

Local Audio Files

When you start a recording, RecordGuard saves audio files locally on your Mac. These files may include system-audio and microphone-audio backup tracks.

These files remain on your device unless you choose to share, move, import, or delete them.

Permissions

RecordGuard asks macOS for Screen & System Audio Recording permission to capture system audio and Microphone permission to capture microphone audio.

Protected or Sensitive Content

RecordGuard is not intended for hidden recording, protected media recording, or recording sensitive content without permission.

Email Feedback and Support

If you choose to email feedback or support details, the information you include in that email will be received through your email provider and handled for support and product-improvement purposes.

Please do not send sensitive audio recordings unless specifically requested.

Third-Party Services

RecordGuard does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be published at this URL.

RecordGuard 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 6 月 25 日

RecordGuard 是一款 Mac 工具，用于在录制流程中创建本地备用音频轨道。本隐私政策说明 RecordGuard 如何处理数据。

数据收集

RecordGuard 不收集个人数据。

本 App 不需要账号，不使用分析服务，不使用广告追踪器，也不会将音频文件上传到服务器。

本地音频文件

当你开始录制时，RecordGuard 会在你的 Mac 本地保存音频文件。这些文件可能包括系统音频和麦克风音频备用轨道。

这些文件会保留在你的设备上，除非你主动分享、移动、导入或删除它们。

权限

RecordGuard 会向 macOS 请求屏幕与系统音频录制权限，以捕获系统音频；也会请求麦克风权限，以捕获麦克风音频。

受保护或敏感内容

RecordGuard 不用于隐蔽录音、受保护媒体录制，或未经许可录制敏感内容。

邮件反馈和支持

如果你选择通过邮件发送反馈或支持信息，你在邮件中包含的信息会通过你的邮件服务提供商发送，并用于支持和产品改进。

除非特别要求，请不要发送敏感音频文件。

第三方服务

RecordGuard 不集成第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。

政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新内容将发布在此网址。
