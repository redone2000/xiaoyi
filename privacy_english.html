一、我们如何收集和使用您的信息 / How We Collect and Use Your Information

听写写无需注册或登录即可使用。我们不会要求您提供手机号、真实姓名、身份证件号码等身份信息。

TingXieXie can be used without registration or login. We do not ask for your phone number, real name, government ID, or similar identity information.

（一）学习内容 / Learning Content

您在应用内创建、导入、编辑或练习的词表、单词、短语、句子、听写结果等学习内容，默认保存在您的设备本地。我们不会将这些学习内容用于公开展示、广告投放或训练公开模型。

Vocabulary lists, words, phrases, sentences, dictation results, and other learning content that you create, import, edit, or practice in the app are stored locally on your device by default. We do not use this learning content for public display, advertising, or training public models.

（二）高清音质云端合成 / High-Quality Cloud Speech Synthesis

当您使用 Pro 高清音质功能时，应用会将需要朗读的文本发送至云端语音合成服务，用于生成更清晰自然的朗读音频。生成后的音频会下载并缓存在您的设备本地，以便后续重复练习时优先使用本地缓存。我们仅为提供该功能而处理相关文本和音频，不会将其用于公开展示或广告目的。

When you use the Pro high-quality audio feature, the app sends the text to be read aloud to a cloud speech synthesis service to generate clearer and more natural audio. The generated audio is downloaded and cached locally on your device so later practice can use the local cache first. We process the relevant text and audio only to provide this feature and do not use it for public display or advertising.

（三）必要的设备、日志与功能统计 / Essential Device, Log, and Usage Information

为保障服务稳定性、排查问题并改进体验，我们可能收集设备型号、系统版本、应用版本、崩溃日志、网络类型，以及匿名的功能使用统计。这些信息仅用于技术支持和产品优化，不包含您的学习内容，且无法单独识别您的身份。

To maintain service stability, troubleshoot issues, and improve the experience, we may collect device model, system version, app version, crash logs, network type, and anonymous feature usage statistics. This information is used only for technical support and product improvement, does not include your learning content, and cannot identify you on its own.

（四）订阅/内购相关信息 / Subscription and In-App Purchase Information

当您通过 App Store 购买订阅或永久解锁时，我们会接收必要的订单票据、订阅状态、到期时间、恢复购买状态等信息，用于开通、续期、恢复或校验您的 Pro 权益。支付由 Apple 处理，我们不存储您的银行卡号或 Apple ID 密码。

When you purchase a subscription or lifetime unlock through the App Store, we receive necessary receipt, subscription status, expiration date, restore status, and related information to activate, renew, restore, or validate your Pro benefits. Payments are processed by Apple, and we do not store your card details or Apple ID password.

二、我们如何共享、转让与公开披露信息 / How We Share, Transfer, and Disclose Information

除非取得您的同意或法律法规要求，我们不会出售、出租或公开披露您的个人信息。为实现应用功能，我们可能在必要范围内与以下服务方处理相关信息：Apple（用于内购、订阅、恢复购买和崩溃诊断）、云端语音合成服务（仅用于高清音质音频生成）、匿名统计或崩溃分析服务（仅用于产品稳定性和体验改进）。

We do not sell, rent, or publicly disclose your personal information unless with your consent or as required by law. To provide app features, we may process necessary information with the following service providers: Apple for in-app purchases, subscriptions, purchase restoration, and crash diagnostics; cloud speech synthesis services solely for high-quality audio generation; and anonymous analytics or crash analysis services solely for stability and experience improvement.

三、您的权利 / Your Rights

您可以在设备上自行管理或删除本地学习内容和缓存音频。您也可以通过系统设置管理 App 权限，通过 App Store 管理或取消订阅。如需咨询、删除与您相关的可识别信息，或撤回非必要统计授权，可通过下方邮箱联系我们。

You can manage or delete local learning content and cached audio on your device. You can also manage app permissions in system settings and manage or cancel subscriptions through the App Store. To request assistance, deletion of identifiable information related to you, or withdrawal of non-essential analytics consent, please contact us by email below.

四、儿童隐私 / Children's Privacy

听写写面向一般英语学习场景，不要求用户提供年龄或身份信息。若监护人认为未成年人向我们提供了个人信息，请联系我们，我们会在核实后协助处理。

TingXieXie is designed for general English learning scenarios and does not require users to provide age or identity information. If a guardian believes that a minor has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will assist after verification.

五、政策更新 / Policy Updates

我们可能根据功能变化、法律法规或平台要求更新本政策。重大变更将通过应用内提示、App Store 页面或本页面更新日期等方式告知。

We may update this Policy based on feature changes, laws and regulations, or platform requirements. Significant changes may be announced through in-app notices, the App Store page, or the updated date on this page.
