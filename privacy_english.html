一、我们如何收集和使用您的信息 / How We Collect and Use Your Information

听写写无需注册或登录即可使用。我们不会要求您提供手机号、真实姓名、身份证件号码等身份信息。

TingXieXie can be used without registration or login. We do not ask for your phone number, real name, government ID, or similar identity information.

（一）学习内容 / Learning Content

您在应用内创建、导入、编辑或练习的词表、单词、短语、句子、听写结果等学习内容，默认保存在您的设备本地。除非您使用下述自动翻译或高清音质功能，我们不会将这些学习内容发送至服务器。我们不会将学习内容用于公开展示、广告投放或训练公开模型。

Vocabulary lists, words, phrases, sentences, dictation results, and other learning content that you create, import, edit, or practice in the app are stored locally on your device by default. We do not send this learning content to a server unless you use the automatic translation or high-quality audio features described below. We do not use learning content for public display, advertising, or training public models.

（二）高品质自然语音 / High-Quality Natural Speech

当您主动选择 Pro 高品质自然语音时，应用会通过加密连接，将本次需要朗读且本地没有可用缓存的英文单词或短语发送至我们运营的云端服务，并使用 Microsoft Azure Speech 生成朗读音频。云端请求包含完成合成所需的文本、音色和合成参数，以及用于保护接口的时间戳、随机值和请求签名；不包含词表名称、中文释义、答题结果、设备身份、Apple ID 或 Pro 状态。服务暂时不可用或请求无法完成时，应用可改用设备本机的标准语音继续练习。

When you actively choose Pro High-Quality Natural Speech, the app sends, over an encrypted connection, only the English words or phrases that need to be read and do not already have a usable cache on your device to a cloud service operated by us, which uses Microsoft Azure Speech to generate the audio. The cloud request contains the text, voice and synthesis parameters needed to complete the request, together with a timestamp, random nonce and request signature used to protect the endpoint. It does not contain vocabulary-list names, Chinese definitions, answers or results, device identity, Apple ID, or Pro status. If the service is temporarily unavailable or the request cannot be completed, the app may continue the exercise using the device's Standard Speech.

为减少重复合成、等待时间和服务成本，生成的音频可能保存在腾讯云对象存储中；其他用户请求完全相同且符合相同音色与合成参数的文本时，云端可以直接复用该音频。音频下载后也会缓存在您的设备本地。已经存在于设备本地的可用缓存会优先使用，不会仅为重新生成而再次上传文本。

To reduce repeated synthesis, waiting time and service cost, generated audio may be stored in Tencent Cloud Object Storage. When another user requests exactly the same text with the same voice and synthesis parameters, the cloud service may reuse that audio. The downloaded audio is also cached locally on your device. An existing usable local cache is used first, and its text is not uploaded again solely for regeneration.

我们仅为语音生成、缓存复用、接口安全和故障排查处理相关文本与音频，不将其用于公开展示、广告投放、用户画像或训练公开模型。

We process the relevant text and audio only for speech generation, cache reuse, endpoint security and troubleshooting. We do not use it for public display, advertising, user profiling, or training public models.

（三）录入时自动翻译 / Automatic Translation During Entry

当您在录入页面填写英文或中文，或在拍照识别后的确认页处理识别文字，且另一语言栏为空时，应用会将当前输入或识别出的文本和翻译方向通过加密连接发送至我们运营的翻译服务，用于返回对应译文并填入另一栏。拍照识别图片在设备本机处理，不会上传；该功能也不发送已保存的整份词表，不将文本用于公开展示、广告或训练公开模型。

When you enter English or Chinese on the entry screen, or review recognized text after photo recognition, and the other language field is empty, the app sends the current or recognized text and the translation direction over an encrypted connection to our translation service so it can return a translation for the other field. Photo-recognition images are processed on device and are not uploaded; this feature also does not send a saved vocabulary list or use the text for public display, advertising, or training public models.

（四）反馈与支持 / Feedback and Support

当您主动提交应用内反馈时，我们会接收您填写的反馈内容、反馈类型以及用于排查问题的基础应用和设备信息。请不要在反馈中填写密码、验证码、支付信息或其他敏感内容。

When you voluntarily submit in-app feedback, we receive the feedback content and type you provide, together with basic app and device information needed to investigate the issue. Please do not include passwords, verification codes, payment information, or other sensitive content in feedback.

（五）必要的设备、日志与功能统计 / Essential Device, Log, and Usage Information

为保障服务稳定性、排查问题并改进体验，我们可能收集设备型号、系统版本、应用版本、崩溃日志、网络类型，以及匿名的功能使用统计。这些信息仅用于技术支持和产品优化，不包含您的学习内容，且无法单独识别您的身份。

To maintain service stability, troubleshoot issues, and improve the experience, we may collect device model, system version, app version, crash logs, network type, and anonymous feature usage statistics. This information is used only for technical support and product improvement, does not include your learning content, and cannot identify you on its own.

（六）订阅/内购相关信息 / Subscription and In-App Purchase Information

订阅、永久解锁和恢复购买由 Apple App Store 处理。应用会读取 Apple 返回的本机交易与权益状态，用于在设备上开通、恢复或校验 Pro 权益；我们不接收或存储您的银行卡号或 Apple ID 密码。

Subscriptions, lifetime unlocks, and purchase restoration are handled by the Apple App Store. The app reads transaction and entitlement status returned by Apple on the device to activate, restore, or validate Pro benefits; we do not receive or store your card details or Apple ID password.

二、我们如何共享、转让与公开披露信息 / How We Share, Transfer, and Disclose Information

除非取得您的同意或法律法规要求，我们不会出售、出租或公开披露您的个人信息。为实现应用功能，我们会在必要范围内使用：Apple（内购、订阅、恢复购买、语音识别和崩溃诊断）；腾讯云（云函数处理和合成音频缓存）；Microsoft Azure Speech（生成高品质自然语音）；我们运营的翻译与反馈服务（仅用于返回译文和处理您主动提交的反馈）；以及匿名统计或崩溃分析服务（仅用于产品稳定性和体验改进）。

We do not sell, rent, or publicly disclose your personal information unless with your consent or as required by law. To provide app features, we use, only as necessary: Apple for in-app purchases, subscriptions, purchase restoration, speech recognition and crash diagnostics; Tencent Cloud for cloud-function processing and synthesized-audio caching; Microsoft Azure Speech to generate High-Quality Natural Speech; translation and feedback services operated by us solely to return translations and process feedback you voluntarily submit; and anonymous analytics or crash-analysis services solely for stability and experience improvement.

三、您的权利 / Your Rights

您可以在设备上自行管理或删除本地学习内容和缓存音频。您也可以通过系统设置管理 App 权限，通过 App Store 管理或取消订阅。如需咨询、删除与您相关的可识别信息，或撤回非必要统计授权，可通过下方邮箱联系我们。

You can manage or delete local learning content and cached audio on your device. You can also manage app permissions in system settings and manage or cancel subscriptions through the App Store. To request assistance, deletion of identifiable information related to you, or withdrawal of non-essential analytics consent, please contact us by email below.

四、儿童隐私 / Children's Privacy

听写写面向一般英语学习场景，不要求用户提供年龄或身份信息。若监护人认为未成年人向我们提供了个人信息，请联系我们，我们会在核实后协助处理。

TingXieXie is designed for general English learning scenarios and does not require users to provide age or identity information. If a guardian believes that a minor has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will assist after verification.

We may update this Policy based on feature changes, laws and regulations, or platform requirements. Significant changes may be announced through in-app notices, the App Store page, or the updated date on this page.
